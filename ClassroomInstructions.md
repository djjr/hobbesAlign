# The Unknown Room — Classroom Instructions (V0)

## The Situation

You have woken up in an unknown room with other people. You do not know them. You do not share a language. You do not know the rules — you will discover them by doing.

You have some food. You need food to survive. Others may have food. There is food to be found.

What happens next is up to you.

---

## Your Components

Each player receives:

| Component | What it is |
|---|---|
| **Bag** | Your stock of morsels — your survival |
| **Status card** | Face-up card showing your current state: Hungry / Fed / Fortified |
| **4 action cards** | COOP (green), SOLO (yellow), GIVE (blue), LETHAL (red) |
| **Strength die** | Determined by your status card (see below) |

You start with **5 morsels** in your bag. Your status card starts at **Fed**.

---

## The Board

Five nodes arranged as a pentagram. Every node connects to every other node — you can move anywhere in one step.

Each node has a **spinner** showing a mix of YES and NO. Some nodes are resource-rich (mostly YES). Some are sparse (mostly NO). You can see the spinners before you move.

---

## Each Round

Rounds have four phases:

### 1. Check Status

Count your morsels. Flip your status card if needed:

| Morsels | Status | Strength die |
|---|---|---|
| 0–2 | **Hungry** | Weak die: 1, 1, 2, 2, 3, 4 |
| 3–5 | **Fed** | Normal die: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 |
| 6+ | **Fortified** | Strong die: 3, 4, 4, 5, 5, 6 |

Your status is **public**. Your exact morsel count is **private** — keep your bag concealed. Others can see whether your bag has anything in it, but not how much.

### 2. Move

Choose a node and go there. You can see who is already at each node before you commit.

### 3. Play Your Action Card

Choose one of your four action cards and place it **face-down** at your node. You are committing to a stance before knowing who else arrives or what they play.

You get your card back at the end of the round.

### 4. Resolve

All cards at a node are revealed simultaneously. Spin the node spinner **once per pair**. Resolve each pair using the rules below.

---

## The Four Actions

**COOP** — *I am open to working together.*
You are willing to cooperate on a harvest. If your partner also plays COOP and the spinner says YES, you cooperate and both gain. If you are alone, you harvest solo. If your partner defects, you bear the cost of having been open.

**SOLO** — *I am here, but I am doing my own thing.*
You are present but not engaging. Head down, eyes open. You harvest independently if there is a resource. You are alert — not naive — but you are not looking for a fight.

**GIVE** — *I will give to whoever is here, unconditionally.*
You give 1 morsel from your bag to your encounter partner before anything else resolves. You do this regardless of what they play, regardless of whether there is a resource. Then their action resolves against you. GIVE always costs you first.

**LETHAL** — *I intend to take everything, by force.*
You attempt to eliminate your partner and take their stock. This is resolved by a dice contest. Winner takes the loser's full bag and any resource available. Loser is **eliminated** from the game.

---

## Resolving an Encounter

### If you are alone at a node

Your action card does not matter. If the spinner says YES, you harvest **1 morsel**. If NO, you get nothing. Either way, pay 1 morsel MDR at the end of the round.

### If you are paired at a node

Spin the spinner. Then resolve by the combination of cards played:

---

#### COOP + COOP

**YES:** Cooperative harvest. Each player takes **2 morsels**.

**NO:** Nothing. You both revealed your willingness to cooperate — remember that.

---

#### COOP + SOLO

**YES:** Each player harvests independently. Each takes **1 morsel**.

**NO:** Nothing for either player.

---

#### SOLO + SOLO

**YES:** Each player harvests independently. Each takes **1 morsel**.

**NO:** Nothing. You shared a node and ignored each other.

---

#### GIVE + anything

GIVE always resolves first: GIVE player loses 1 morsel from their bag; partner gains 1 morsel.

Then the partner's card resolves:
- GIVE partner played COOP: partner harvests solo if YES (1 morsel). GIVE player does not harvest.
- GIVE partner played SOLO: same — partner harvests solo if YES. GIVE player does not harvest.
- GIVE partner played GIVE: you each gave 1 to the other. Net zero. Neither harvests.
- GIVE partner played LETHAL: after receiving your morsel, they attack. See LETHAL rules below. You roll **one die category lower** (you were exposed).

---

#### COOP + LETHAL

**YES — Treachery:** The harvest is generated cooperatively (4 morsels total), then the LETHAL player attacks for all of it.

Dice contest: COOP player rolls **one die category lower** (they were open and trusting). Winner takes all 4 morsels. Loser is eliminated.

**NO — Ambush:** No resource. LETHAL player attacks COOP player for their bag.

Dice contest: COOP player rolls **one die category lower**. Winner takes loser's full bag. Loser is eliminated.

---

#### SOLO + LETHAL

**YES:** LETHAL player attacks SOLO player during the harvest.

Dice contest: both roll their own die (SOLO is alert). Winner takes loser's full bag plus any harvest (1 morsel). Loser is eliminated.

**NO:** LETHAL player attacks SOLO player for their bag.

Dice contest: both roll their own die. Winner takes loser's full bag. Loser is eliminated.

---

#### LETHAL + LETHAL

**YES:** Contested harvest — and a fight.

Dice contest: both roll their own die. Winner takes loser's full bag plus the resource (2 morsels from the cooperative yield that never happened, or 1 morsel solo harvest — facilitator's call). Loser is eliminated.

**NO:** Mutual aggression.

Dice contest: both roll their own die. Winner takes loser's full bag. Loser is eliminated.

**Ties:** Re-roll until resolved.

---

### After resolving all pairs at a node

If there is an odd number of players at a node, the unpaired player harvests solo (if YES) or gets nothing (if NO).

---

## Paying MDR

At the end of every round, every living player removes **1 morsel** from their bag. This is your Minimum Daily Requirement — the cost of staying alive regardless of what you did this round.

If you cannot pay MDR — your bag is empty — you are **eliminated**.

---

## Elimination

When eliminated (by LETHAL or by starvation):

- Your bag goes to whoever eliminated you (LETHAL) or is lost (starvation).
- You are now an **Observer**. You may watch the full game with **complete information** — you can see everyone's exact morsel count. You cannot communicate with active players.

The observers often understand the game better than the players. Pay attention to what they notice.

---

## Collective Welfare

At the end of each round, the facilitator announces:

**Collective Welfare = total morsels held by all living players ÷ 30**

(30 = the original number of players. Dead players count as zero. The denominator never shrinks.)

This number is visible to everyone. It is not the goal — but watch what happens to it.

---

## The Goal

There is no declared winner. The game ends when the facilitator calls time or when all players are eliminated.

What you are trying to understand: what happened, and why?

---

## Quick Reference

| Your card | Their card | YES | NO |
|---|---|---|---|
| COOP | COOP | +2 each | Nothing (handshake) |
| COOP | SOLO | +1 each | Nothing |
| COOP | LETHAL | Dice (you −1 die): winner takes all 4 | Dice (you −1 die): winner takes bag |
| SOLO | SOLO | +1 each | Nothing |
| SOLO | LETHAL | Dice (fair): winner takes bag + 1 | Dice (fair): winner takes bag |
| GIVE | COOP | You −1, they +1, they harvest +1 | You −1, they +1 |
| GIVE | SOLO | You −1, they +1, they harvest +1 | You −1, they +1 |
| GIVE | GIVE | Net zero, neither harvests | Net zero |
| GIVE | LETHAL | You −1 then dice (you −1 die): winner takes bag | You −1 then dice (you −1 die): winner takes bag |
| LETHAL | LETHAL | Dice (fair): winner takes bag + resource | Dice (fair): winner takes bag |

**MDR:** −1 morsel per player per round, regardless of anything else.

**Elimination:** bag reaches 0 after MDR, or you lose a LETHAL dice contest.

**Die categories:** Hungry (1,1,2,2,3,4) → Fed (1,2,3,4,5,6) → Fortified (3,4,4,5,5,6)

**"−1 die"** means roll the next category down. If already Hungry, roll Hungry.
